All About Mazao

Created by a farmer for farmers, Mazao's mission is to promote the goodness of buying local, supporting the community, and enjoying the freshest, tastiest fruits and vegetables Kenya has to offer. So why settle for bland supermarket produce when you can savor the flavor of farm-fresh Mazao?

Delivery and Logistics

We work with local logistics companies to ensure swift and reliable delivery of produce from farms to buyers. From field to fork, we make sure every bite is a taste of Kenyan goodness.

Features

More features getting added ...

Product listings:

Farmers can create listings for their produce, including photos, descriptions, and prices. Buyers can browse and search the listings to find what they want.

Reviews and ratings:

Buyers can leave reviews and ratings for the products and the farmers, helping to build trust and credibility in the marketplace.

Payment processing:

We have integrated a payment processor to allow buyers to pay for their orders online. This helps to streamline the purchasing process and reduce the risk of fraud.

Analytics and reporting:

We provide farmers with insights into their sales performance, including data on which products are selling well, which buyers are repeat customers, and which marketing channels are most effective.
